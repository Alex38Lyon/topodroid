Shot edit dialog (1)

The "Shot edit" dialog is opened when you tap a shot entry in the "Shot list" window.

The dialod displays

  • length, azimuth, inclination. Editable for manually entered shots; not for downloaded shots
  • differences from the average acceleration, magnetic field, and magnetic dip
  • shot stations
  • left arrow: load previous leg
  • reverse: swap stations
  • right arrow: load next leg
  • shot comment
  • profile "extend" checkboxes: "left", "vertical", "right"
  • a line with six buttons.
    Shot flags: duplicate and surface.
    Repeated leg check: the shot belongs to the preceeding leg.
    Next leg check: the shot belogs to the following leg.
    Splay group check: apply the station change to all splays in the group of this.
    Renumber check: renumber shots from this onward.
  • OK: confirm the changes and close the dialog.
  • Save: confirm the changes without closing the dialog.
