Bluetooth

This dialog is opened tapping the Bluetooth button in the Shot list window or in the Sketch window.

  • Reset the BT connection
  • turn on/off the laser
  • take a shot and download it
  • take three shots and download them
If you experience problems with the DistoX after downloading some data, you may try to reset the bluetooth connection on TopoDroid, switch the DistoX on and off, and retry downloading the data.
