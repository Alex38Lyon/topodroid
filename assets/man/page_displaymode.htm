Survey data display mode

The Display button in the "shot list" window opens a dialog with choices for which shots to diaplay in the list and how:
  • shot id
  • splay shots
  • blank shots
  • repeated leg shots

The shot ids are unique increasing indices assigned by TopoDroid to the shots of the survey.

Splay shots can be shown or hidden. If the splay shots are hidden it is still possible to show the splay shots for an individual station. Tapping on the station name in a leg shot, makes the splay shots at that station visible. Tapping again on the station name (even in a splay shot) hides them.

Blank shots can be either all shown or hidden.

repeated-leg shots can be either shown or hidden.
