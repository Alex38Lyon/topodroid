Calibration-check shots

This dialog is opened tapping the Calib. check menu in the Survey window.

The dialog shows the calibration check legs. Tapping on a leg-shot it displays the distribution of the angle differences between the shots of the leg and the average leg direction. If there is the opposite leg, the distribution of the angles between any two shots, one for each leg, is also displayed.
The yellow line is 0.5 degrees and the red line is 1.0 degree.
