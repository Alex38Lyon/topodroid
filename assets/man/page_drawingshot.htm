Sketch shot dialog

The "Sketch shot" dialog is opened when you select a survey shot and tap the "Item edit" button (the "note and pencil" icon) in the "Sketch" window (in "edit" mode).

This dialog displays

  • the shot stations in the title
  • the shot data, length, azimuth, and inclination
  • two text field to edit the shot stations
  • a text field for the shot comment
  • three checkboxes for the profile "extend": "left", "vertical", or "right"
  • two checkbuttons for the shot flags: duplicate and surface
  • The Save button to confirm the changes.

Use the "Back" key to close the dialog without saving the changes.
