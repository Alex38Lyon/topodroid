Most recent tools dialog

The "Most-recent tools" dialog displays the symbols of the most recently used points, lines and areas on three rows.

You select a tool by tapping its symbol. A long tap on a symbol brings it in front of the list andi its name is shown on the title.

Each row has a button to the right to go to the list of all tools, so that you can choose one of the others.

The slider on top adjusts the orientation of the first point tool, if this is orientable.
