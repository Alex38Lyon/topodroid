Photo list dialog

The "Photo list" dialog is opned tapping the "Photo" button (the "camera" icon) in the "Survey info" window.

The dialog display the list of photos of the survey.
For each photo it gives

  • The count
  • the shot where to which photo was assigned (shown as the two shot stations)
  • the photo comment

Tapping on an entry in the list opens the "Photo" dialog.
